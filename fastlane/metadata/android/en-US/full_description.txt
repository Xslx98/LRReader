LR Reader is a feature-rich Android client for LANraragi, a self-hosted manga/archive management server.

Features:
• Full-featured search — keyword, category filter, sort, random
• High-performance reader — native C image decoder + smart preloading
• Offline download — background archive download with resume support
• Tag translation — auto-translate tags in Chinese (EhTagDatabase)
• Archive rating — emoji-based star rating system
• Category management — browse, create, edit LANraragi categories
• Secure authentication — encrypted API Key storage
• Multi-server — configure and switch between multiple LANraragi instances
• Multi-language — 10 languages including CJK, English, French, German, Spanish, Thai
• Dark mode — follows system theme
• Upload & URL download — add archives directly from the app

Built upon the EhViewer reading framework.
